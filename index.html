PAINTLESS DENT REPAIR BENEFITS

Conventional dent repair fills vehicle body dents in with paint filler, which is usually matched to the rest of the vehicle’s paint exterior. Although this process will indeed make a vehicle look brand new, it doesn’t address the vehicle’s dent in a way that is a long-term solution. It’s also very time-consuming, expensive and can negatively impact re-sale value of your vehicle.

With Paintless Dent Removal (PDR), there are no fillers or sanding. PDR is an environmentally friendly repair method that restores your vehicle’s body to factory-like conditions in a fraction of the time and less costly. PDR can be used to remove dings and dents from vehicles, as well as other blemishes like creases and hail damage.
